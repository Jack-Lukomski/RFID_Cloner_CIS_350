Welcome to RFID Cloner Central!


This website is used in conjunction with an ESP32 controller. With both the controller and the website, you are able to connect to the ESP32 over Bluetooth Low Engergy. While connected over BLE, you can read an RFID badge and the tag will be held in the websites inclusive storage.

Steps for Cloning

1. Click (Find Bluetooth Devices!); Select your ESP32 device!

2. Put your device near your RFID badge, and click (Scan Badge)

3. Any tag you scan will be held in the dropdown menu!
